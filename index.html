⚙️ The Exam Center Process

Present your passport to the test administrator. They will verify your identity and have you sign a digital agreement.

They will take a digital photograph of you and a scan of your palm vein pattern. This is used to check you in and out of the testing room for breaks. You will be asked to empty your pockets.

You will be assigned a small locker to store all of your belongings (bag, phone, watch, wallet, water, snacks, etc.). Your phone must be turned off completely.

The administrator will provide you with a laminated booklet (your "scratchpad") and special pens. They will then escort you to your assigned computer.

The administrator will log you in, and you will go through a short tutorial before your first section begins.

When a section ends, your optional 10-minute break begins. You must raise your hand. The administrator will come and escort you out. You will need to scan your palm vein to exit and re-enter. You can access your locker during this time but must be back in your seat before the 10 minutes are up.
